Chemistry

Chemistry is the branch of science that studies matter — what things are made of, how they are structured, their properties, and how they change during reactions.

Branches Of Chemistry

Organic Chemistry

  • Study of compounds that contain carbon
  • Examples: fuels, plastics, medicines, proteins
  • Basically the chemistry of living things and carbon-based materials
